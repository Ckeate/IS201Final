About Me

My name is Caden Keate, I am from Fort Collins, Colorado. I love the Outdoors, specifically Mountain Biking and Skiing. I'm currently attending BYU and I am a Finance Major with a subfocus on Investment Banking. I was first exposed to Finance when I was about 8 years old by my uncle who was working at YUM! Brand Foods at the time in their Finance Department. I've worked several jobs as a Service Technician in local bike shops, having to identify problems with bicycles and then repair them, as well as working with customers creating repair plans that fit their needs as a cyclist and budget. In my several years of working in Bicycle service I have grown to love the challenge of solving problems and the dynamic and changing work environment. In an industry that is constantly innovating I love the challenge of learning new techniques to repair new and innovative products. Something I love about bicycle service is the teamwork, a problem that one mechanic can't solve is a problem none of us can solve. So frequently we collaborate, and work together learning to fix new things and solve countless unique problems.

As I mentioned I love to ski and ride mountain bikes. Something I love about these sports is they give me the opportunity to push my self and my limits by overcoming my fears and trying something until I get it, whether it's a trick or a particularly sketchy section of trail. One of my favorite riders and videos is Matt Jones pushing the limits. Matt is a slopestyle rider from England and he does a lot of things that seem impossible on a bike. So click on the link and enjoy one of my all time favorite videos.
